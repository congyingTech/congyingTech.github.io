leetcode:373--Find K Pairs with Smallest Sums

发表于 2016-07-11   |   分类于 Python ， leetcode ， jobs   |  

题目描述：
Given nums1 = [1,1,2], nums2 = [1,2,3], k = 2

Return: [1,1],[1,1]

The first 2 pairs are returned from the sequence:
[1,1],[1,1],[1,2],[2,1],[1,2],[2,2],[1,3],[1,3],[2,3]
